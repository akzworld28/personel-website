About

"Home is where the heart is, but the world is where my heart dreams to be." Living in Tamil Nadu, I’m fueled by a passion for exploring new places and making lifelong connections along the way. With BCA and MBA degrees, I’m driven by curiosity, positivity, and a love for building meaningful relationships. A vegetarian and an avid cook, I find joy in creating dishes that bring people together. For me, life is all about laughter, loved ones, and the endless adventures that await!

Trips

Varkala

"The sea whispers secrets, and Varkala listens."

Varkala’s cliffs and beaches left me speechless. The view from the top was a reminder of how small we are in this vast world, yet how deeply we are connected to it.

Kanyakumari

"Where the oceans meet, so do our dreams."

Standing at the southernmost tip of India, I felt like I was at the edge of the world, and the view of the confluence of three oceans was nothing short of magical.

Kovalam

"Where the waves meet the sky, Kovalam’s beaches tell a story of tranquility."

Kovalam’s golden sands and relaxed vibe made it the perfect place to unwind. Watching the sunset over the Arabian Sea felt like the world slowing down just for me.

Ooty

"Ooty is nature’s canvas, painted in every shade of green."

Ooty’s cool climate and lush tea gardens made me fall in love with its peaceful vibe. Walking through the gardens felt like strolling in a postcard.

Kodaikanal

"In the mist of Kodaikanal, I found clarity."

Kodaikanal’s misty landscapes and serene lakes made me feel like I had stepped into a dream. It was the perfect retreat for some soul-searching and reflection.

Shimla

"Shimla’s charm lies not just in its snow-capped peaks, but in its timeless elegance."

Shimla’s colonial buildings and cool weather made it a perfect escape. The feeling of walking on Mall Road was like stepping into a bygone era.

Delhi

"Delhi isn’t just a city; it’s a collision of history and modernity."

From the grandeur of the Red Fort to the bustling streets of Connaught Place, Delhi is where every step feels like walking through time.

Punjab

"Punjab is where the heart finds its rhythm, and the soul finds its melody."

From the Golden Temple’s serenity to the vibrant fields, Punjab filled my heart with warmth. It’s a place where tradition meets joy in the most beautiful ways.

Agra

(Taj Mahal)

"The Taj Mahal isn’t just a monument; it’s a symbol of eternal love."

Seeing the Taj Mahal up close was an experience beyond words. The craftsmanship and history of this incredible structure made me reflect on the power of love and legacy.
